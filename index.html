About

I started coding websites with design experiments, and from there was hired by a local ISP as their Webmaster. It was a full-spectrum introduction into web development, from crafting websites to running the web-hosting department.

I've carried this creativity, flexibility and versatility into every role since. I'm grateful to have work in technical and creative functions which prepared me for this hybrid career where i'm part graphic-designer, front-end developer, ux designer and multimedia specialist

When I'm not coding, I enjoy writing music, art and gaming.
